The Solutions
Evolve to a workflow-based structure.
Function-based: work organized by role. Everyone owns a piece, no one owns the outcome.

Workflow-based: work organized by outcome. Engineered progression. Accountability at every stage.

Now you can tell exactly who is accountable when there's an issue in the line.

Solution 1

The Elite Case Manager Task Force.

The core operational engine of BJB's pre-lit rebuild, settlement growth, and national expansion strategy.

1. The Operational Bridge

The missing middle between case acquisition and settlement conversion. Without this bridge, volume accumulates faster than value.

  • Case Development qualifies and activates
  • Elite CM Task Force builds and advances
  • Settlement resolves the engineered file
2. Stop Case Decay

Aged cases don't just cost more — they become worth less. Treatment gaps, client disengagement, weakened causation, fragmented records.

  • Every case assigned a progression owner
  • Treatment gaps surfaced in days, not months
  • A slow case is often a smaller case
3. National Expansion Engine

Centralized from Hackensack. Standardized workflows, offshore admin, remote treatment oversight. Enter new states without duplicating infrastructure.

  • Centralized operational control
  • Offshore handles repeatable admin work
  • Makes national expansion economically possible
4. The Economics

Not paying for more staff. Buying throughput, value per case, speed to settlement, and national scalability.

  • 37 more cases/week to RFD × $15K = $555K/week
  • $555K × 52 = $28.86M/year

Solution 2

The Strategic Resolution Group.

Not replacing the current resolution team. They keep the big stuff — everything over $50K. SRG handles everything under $50K independently.

1. Settlement Strike Force

Does everything necessary to settle cases — except file a complaint. Liability analysis, treatment analysis, damages evaluation, expert coordination, negotiation strategy.

  • 2 Senior Settlement Strategists (10+ yr)
  • 2 Settlement Pursuit Specialists
  • Lean, high-performance, rapid deployment
2. Strategic Negotiation Cadence

Not passive follow-up. A structured pressure campaign — every touchpoint has a purpose.

  • Day 0: Anchor value with strong demand
  • Day 3: Confirm receipt + set expectations
  • Day 7-10: Reinforce, request first offer
  • Day 14: Escalate negotiation
  • Day 21: Evidence drip strategy
  • Day 30: Supervisor escalation
3. Production Capacity

This small team moves fast.

  • 160 demands/week — 640/month
  • Each Pursuit Specialist: 400-600 active demands
  • 30 min per demand prep (AI-assisted, templates)
  • Pre-Lit must supply 160 settlement-ready cases/week
4. Advantage Platform

Performance tracking that becomes a competitive advantage.

  • Settlement velocity tracking per adjuster
  • Adjuster intelligence: who settles fast, who undervalues
  • Negotiation ladder with offer tracking
  • Pipeline visibility — nothing stalls
