The Solutions
Evolve to a workflow-based structure.
Function-based: work organized by role. Everyone owns a piece, no one owns the outcome.

Workflow-based: work organized by outcome. Engineered progression. Accountability at every stage.
Solution 1

The Elite Case Manager Task Force.

The core operational engine of BJB's pre-lit rebuild, settlement growth, and national expansion strategy.

1. The Operational Bridge

The missing middle between case acquisition and settlement conversion. Without this bridge, volume accumulates faster than value.

  • Case Development qualifies and activates
  • Elite CM Task Force builds and advances
  • Settlement resolves the engineered file
2. Stop Case Decay

Aged cases don't just cost more — they become worth less. Treatment gaps, client disengagement, weakened causation, fragmented records.

  • Every case assigned a progression owner
  • Treatment gaps surfaced in days, not months
  • A slow case is often a smaller case
3. National Expansion Engine

Centralized from Hackensack. Standardized workflows, offshore admin, remote treatment oversight. Enter new states without duplicating infrastructure.

  • Centralized operational control
  • Offshore handles repeatable admin work
  • Makes national expansion economically possible
4. The Economics

Not paying for more staff. Buying throughput, value per case, speed to settlement, and national scalability.

  • 37 more cases/week to RFD × $15K = $555K/week
  • $555K × 52 = $28.86M/year
  • 55 Elite CMs needed (retain 20, hire 35)
  • Comp: $65K salary + $65K performance bonus
