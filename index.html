The Solutions
Evolve to a workflow-based structure.
Function-based: work organized by role. Everyone owns a piece, no one owns the outcome.

Workflow-based: work organized by outcome. Engineered progression. Accountability at every stage.
Solution 1

Recruit, hire, train, and deploy an Elite Case Manager Task Force.

The Build
  • 55 Case Engineers needed (3,800 ÷ 70 files)
  • Retain 20 current CMs, hire 35 additional
  • Two groups: MMLs (rebuilt) + CMs (elite tier)
  • This department must be built, not renamed
Compensation
  • $65K salary + $65K performance bonus
  • Comp tied to throughput and case value
  • Performance-based culture from day one
  • Attract high-caliber talent
How to Find Them
  • Targeted recruiting from competing PI firms
  • Legal staffing agencies specializing in PI
  • Clear job description emphasizing production role
  • Filter for treatment coordination experience
How to Interview
  • Scenario-based assessment: case progression
  • Evaluate treatment coordination instincts
